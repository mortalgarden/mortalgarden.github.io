introduction

hello, hello; welcome! i doubt you found this organically. if you did... congrats? this site is just where i dump all of my autism related to the magnus archives and the magnus protocol, mostly for myself, because i resent google docs and things of the sort; decided to just code something oriented toward this. i guess it's a little spintrest project. this will slowly build up as i go through and re-listen to/reread the transcripts for the TMA episodes, as well as the TMagP episodes that have been—and continue to—come out.

this site will contain spoilers! this is your one and only warning! turn back now if you just happened to come across this and don't want spoilers to these podcasts!

lastly, this is not a wiki! i am documenting my thoughts, analyses, and sometimes opinions on the podcast and it's contents here! if you are looking for a wiki, i reccommend going here for TMA and here for TMagP!
