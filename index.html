Introduction

a personal web-project i've made to document my thoughts and analyses on the magnus archives and the magnus protocol; the analyses will be primarily focused on the episodes and the fears, however i may go in-and-out of delving into the characters.

this is mostly for myself, as someone who resents google docs and things of the sort; decided to just code something myself with the sole purpose of being where i lay out these thoughts. contents of the site will slowly build up as i go through my TMA relisten/transcript reread, as well as the TMagP seasons that have been‍—and continue to‍— come out.

if you happen to come upon this site, this will be your one and only warning that this site will contain spoilers for both the magnus archives and protocol! turn back now if you don't want spoilers to these podcasts!

and lastly, this is not a wiki! although some information, at least for now, is taken directly from the wiki, this is not made with the intention of being any sort of wiki for the magnusverse. if you are looking for a wiki, i reccommend going this TMA wiki and this TMagP wiki; other great resources, this time for transcripts, are this TMA transcript archive and this TMagP transcript archive.

Non Vacillabimus.
