Introduction

this is a slightly modified rendition of strangecreetures' chart of generic themes shared by each Entity; these modifications are mostly just so it's dark-mode friendly, as well as changing up the colors. i find this to be a fairly useful resource in narrowing down vague appearances of the Fears; although they are much more pronounced in the magnus archives, i may be using these themes in some way for the magnus protocol, at least until we learn more about the Entities in that reality.

this is a slightly modified rendition of strangecreetures' chart of generic themes shared by each Entity; these modifications are mostly just so it's dark-mode friendly, as well as changing up the colors. i find this to be a fairly useful resource in narrowing down vague appearances of the Fears; although they are much more pronounced in the magnus archives, i may be using these themes in some way for the magnus protocol, at least until we learn more about the Entities in that reality.

the colors represent these themes:

Abstract inevitability (something bad will happen‍—but what?) Things that lurk Inherent flaw (you are a bad person, you are unlovable, etc.) Invasive closeness/intimacy Concrete inevitability (conveyor belt effect‍—you know exactly what's coming for you) Dehumanizing & oppressive systems (late‑stage capitalism, factory farms, etc.) Visible, physical threat Instability‍—the threat of sudden & unpredictable change Mutilation Guilt Lack of closeness/intimacy Unstoppable forces of nature (death, time, etc.) Eternal stagnation
