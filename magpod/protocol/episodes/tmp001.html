Report Locations: Grantham Cemetery

Report Summary:
This report follows an email from Harriet (hpw332@mailpod.com) to someone named ‘Darla’ (dwinny@mailpod.com), who can be assumed to be her family member due to sharing surnames. Harriet had been speaking with a consultant (most likely a grief consultant), whose name was never mentioned, as she was mourning the loss of Arthur, her late partner of 20 years. The consultant told Harriet to go to Grantham Cemetery over the phone one night; She mentions how he sounded excited in an off-putting way on that phone call, which was unlike him. At the cemetery, instead of the consultant, she finds ‘Arthur’; A discolored, mismatched, undead version of Arthur. It sounded just like Arthur, and it recognized Harriet. When asked, it responded that it was ‘some of’ Arthur. She runs, and doesn’t get followed. Harriet asks Darla if she’s free, as she’s afraid of staying at her home due to her newfound fear of Arthur.

Entity Influence:
Primarily The End; seen in the death, graveyard/cemetery, and [partial] reanimation of Arthur. Potentially mixed with The Stranger; hints of it are within the suddenly off-putting behavior of the consultant, ‘Arthur’s features being mismatched, mentions of his shape being ‘wrong’, something having potentially been pressing against his skin from the inside, and ‘Arthur’ saying “Some of him.” in response to Harriet.
